Designed and built in Kalamazoo Michigan

Invented by Michigan native, Alan Williams, The Joiner is is essentially a circular saw and router guide. Its unique design is flexible enough to accommodate board widths from 1" x 2" to 1" x 12" (up to 24" widths with optional extension arms). It can be easily adjusted for most circular saws and routers. Additionally, The Joiner ® can be used as a router guide with many applications.
In preparation, the two boards are installed in their desired, final position, overlapping each other where the joint is to be made. No removal or reinstallation is ever needed for adjustments.
Then, using the Joiner ® is as easy as 1 - 2 - 3!
